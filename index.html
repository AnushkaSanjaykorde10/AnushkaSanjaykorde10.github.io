My approach to design

01

It starts with the people

I dig into the people and the business first — talking to founders and stakeholders, mapping flows, and finding where users actually get stuck.

02

Explore fast, cut early

With Claude and Figma I try many directions quickly and drop the weak ones early — keeping only the path that takes friction off real people.

03

Systems keep it calm

I build the design systems and interaction details that keep everything consistent, so the experience stays calm and clear as it scales.
